We are Python Dev Society

Welcome To Python Dev Society

Python Dev Society is a project in which everyone, from a professional to an amateur in programming, can exchange experiences, ideas and opportunities through our educational mission.

Welcome

Mission and Vision for the future

Python Dev Society is a community of people who share an interest in programming with one of the most popular languages ​​in the world - Python. Within a year of its founding, we managed to gain thousands of followers on our news channels.The main goal of the Python Dev Society is to unite from beginners to advanced professionals, to help develop their knowledge and skills through our educational program and to spark interest in programming in thousands of people around the world.

Read more

Our mission

The main mission of the Python Dev Society is to increase interest in the Python language, to provide training and development to both novice programmers and people with experience in programming. Our top speakers through our mentoring program will contribute to your development in the world of programming with the most popular programming language for 2022 - Python
